The Best Movies According to Nikita

My top 4 movies of all-time.

The Hobbit Series

This is a trilogy based on J.R.R. Tolkien's novel The Hobbit. Directed by Peter Jackson, the films (An Unexpected Journey, The Desolation of Smaug, and The Battle of the Five Armies) follow Bilbo Baggins as he embarks on an epic adventure with a group of dwarves to reclaim their homeland from the dragon Smaug.

Harry Potter Series

Based on J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter books, this iconic film series follows the journey of Harry Potter, a young wizard destined to defeat the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. Spanning eight movies, it showcases friendship, magic, and the battle between good and evil.

The Chronicles of Narnia

Adapted from C.S. Lewis’s fantasy novels, this film series brings to life the magical land of Narnia, where children from the real world discover their destiny in battles against evil. Notable films include The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe, Prince Caspian, and The Voyage of the Dawn Treader.

Resident Evil Series

Based on the popular video game series, these action-horror movies follow Alice (played by Milla Jovovich) as she fights against the Umbrella Corporation and its zombie-inducing virus. The series blends intense action with sci-fi horror elements.

The Avengers Series

A part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), The Avengers films bring together Earth's mightiest heroes—Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye—against powerful enemies
